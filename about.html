Who We Are
More than engineering. It is lasting impact.

Engineers Without Borders at UMBC is a student-led chapter dedicated to building sustainable solutions for disadvantaged communities while developing globally aware engineers and leaders.

Our Values
  • Community-centered thinking - needs and voices of partners guide our work.
  • Cultural humility - learning from the communities we serve.
  • Sustainability - solutions that are environmentally responsible and long-lasting.
  • Accountability - taking ownership of our designs and their real-world impact.
  • Growth and learning - curiosity, reflection, and continuous improvement.

Our Four Goals

01Improve quality of life for disadvantaged communities
02Advance economic and environmental sustainability
03Create opportunities for students to get involved
04Foster professional development for UMBC students
Mission and Approach
How we work.
Our mission is to partner with communities to design and implement sustainable, community-driven engineering solutions while empowering UMBC students to grow professionally and develop as effective leaders.
01

Holding weekly meetings to collaborate, plan, and develop both technical and professional skills

02

Engaging in team-based collaboration across different fields of study

03

Focusing on appropriate and accessible technologies that can be maintained locally

04

Prioritizing long-term thinking, including operation, maintenance, and sustainability

05

Integrating learning with action through real-world challenges

06

Open to students of all majors - no engineering background required to get involved
