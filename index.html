Who We Are
More than engineering — it's lasting impact.

Engineers Without Borders at UMBC is a student-led chapter dedicated to building sustainable solutions for disadvantaged communities while developing globally aware engineers and leaders.

Our Values
  • Community-centered thinking — needs and voices of partners guide our work.
  • Cultural humility — learning from the communities we serve.
  • Sustainability — solutions that are environmentally responsible and long-lasting.
  • Accountability — taking ownership of our designs and their real-world impact.
  • Growth and learning — curiosity, reflection, and continuous improvement.

Our Four Goals

01Improve quality of life for disadvantaged communities
02Advance economic and environmental sustainability
03Create opportunities for students to get involved
04Foster professional development for UMBC students
